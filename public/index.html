About

A new chapter in Arab media

Award-winning Arab presenter Rania Barghout has launched a new media company, The Next Chapter, offering podcasts, production, media training, and public speaking development services.

The company's first release is No2ta A Al Satr, a bold Arabic podcast that marks Barghout's return to the spotlight and sets the tone for the kind of original, purpose‑driven content The Next Chapter aims to produce.

With decades of on‑air experience across regional and international networks, Barghout's new platform is designed to help individuals and brands share their stories with clarity, confidence, and impact.
